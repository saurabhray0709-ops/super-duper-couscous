About Me

The Wild Monk

Capturing the raw beauty of nature through my lens. With years of experience tracking and photographing wildlife across India's diverse ecosystems - from the grasslands of Kaziranga to the Western Ghats - I bring you intimate moments from the wild that tell stories of survival, beauty, and the delicate balance of nature.

My work focuses on the majestic big game of India - Asian elephants, one-horned rhinos, and the mighty gaur - alongside the vibrant birdlife that inhabits these landscapes. Through my workshops and expeditions, I share not just photography techniques, but a deep reverence for the natural world and its incredible inhabitants.
